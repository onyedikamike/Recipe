Afang Soup

Description

Afang soup is a tasty, nutritious Nigerian soup, native to the Efiks/Ibibios (Akwa Ibom & Cross river states).

Ingredients

  • 500g Palm Fruits or 400g tinned Palm Fruit Concentrate Assorted meat and fish. You can use: Beef or Goat Meat Cow Skin (ponmo) Smoked fish Dry fish Stock fish 1 handful thinly sliced atama leaves 1 onion 2 tablespoons ground crayfish 2 milk cups unshelled periwinkles Habanero pepper (Atarodo, ose oyibo, atarugu: to taste) 2 small stock cubes Salt (to taste)
