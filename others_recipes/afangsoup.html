Afang Soup

Description

Afang soup is a tasty, nutritious Nigerian soup, native to the Efiks/Ibibios (Akwa Ibom & Cross river states).

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Fish
  • Palm oil
  • Crayfish
  • Pepper
  • Shaki (cow tripe)
  • Waterleaf
  • Okazi leaf
  • Onion
  • Periwinkle
  • Salt
  • Some seasoning
